# work
•	Data exploration of Black Friday sales data to determine important customer attributes for data analysis and prediction of future purchases.
•	Used Association Rule mining to find relationship between customer attribute and their purchases.
•	Decision Tree algorithm, K-means clustering, and hierarchical clustering is used to predict purchases based on customer attributes.
•	This analysis helps businesses to improve their sales by increasing the stock of most bought products and also helps to focus on prospective customers when store is usually under-staffed during Black Friday rush hours
